F1 Track Challenge

How to Play

You are behind the wheel of an F1 car navigating a real race circuit. At each corner, you must decide to go Left or Right.

  • Each corner has a 20-second fuel timer — decide before it runs out or it's game over.
  • Go the wrong direction and you crash out. Game over!
  • Run out of time and you run out of gas on track.
  • Navigate every corner correctly to finish P1!

Tip: Think about the real track layout — some corners you may already know!
